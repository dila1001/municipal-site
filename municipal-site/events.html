KLICELS

[2019-07-27]:The 13th Kuala Lumpur International Communication, Education, Language and Social Science Conference

The conference will bring together scholars and practitioners who interested to present their papers in area of communication, education, language, social science and other related topics. This opportunity could be used as a way to broadening their international networks. For details of research areas, please look at major field of research. All papers must be submitted online and are subject to blind-peer review of our committee. Rewards are provided for best papers and all presented papers will be published in a proceeding with ISBN. We will provide you a letter of acceptance or invitation letter for your visa application or financial sponsorship from your institution. Therefore, you are encouraged to submit your paper at the earliest time possible.

Today's Baby Expo

[2019-07-26]:To celebrate Today's Baby Expo's (Formerly known as Mom & Baby Expo) 10th Anniversary, Today's Baby Expo is rolling out our loyalty program aptly called Today's Baby Rewards Points. Shoppers can now collect points from every Today's Baby Expo nation tour 2019. With every RM1 spent, shoppers will be rewarded with 1 point. The more you spend, the more rewards points you will accumulate. You can then use your rewards points to redeem your favourite items at Today's Baby Expo's redemption counter. What's more, if you can accumulate your rewards points within 2 years, you will have one million redemption products awaiting you to take home!
