Restaurants

Wong solo

The visit will not be complete without a taste of its signature dish, Nasi Ayam Penyet. What makes it special is the 'sambal' which is spicy yet tasty. Try its other dishes too such as fried calamari and avocado juice. Simply marvellous!

Red Wok

Spacious and comfortable place for family dinner. The restuarant has an open concept, where you will experience the fresh air, without any smokers or strong smell from the kitchen. It is a huge space which can easily cater for a big groups. The self service steam boat has an abundance of choice, to please any steam boat fan.
